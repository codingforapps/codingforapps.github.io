What its about.

This game is about you, a rock, you must jump past object adn get to the end of the level. As you go the levels will get progressivly harder and with it being longer. The map/location will start to change as you make you're way up the levels going Through Rainforests, Tundras, Volcanoes, Atlantas, and the final location the Spoopy Savanna.

Features

  • 5 Different Map locations.
  • Customizable Rocks.
  • Mini additing arcade game.
  • Time waister.
