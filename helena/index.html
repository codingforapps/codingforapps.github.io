How to Get Rid of Birds

Birds can be pests so here are some way to get rid of them.

Begone Birds

Birds can be a real problem from pooping on cars to ruining gardens, or just being flat out annoying.Here are a couple ways to rid your backyard, garden, ect. of birds. These ways vary from capturing them or just doing a little planting to get rid of these pests.

  1. Black birds ,a common bird are afraid of the color yellow so, planting yellow flowers in a backyard or garden should help.
  2. Setting traps is always an efficent way of getting rid of birds. (here's a link on how to catch them) https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xREL1bkaOMM
  3. Starlings,another common bird are startled by mirrors and flashing lights so having those wouldn't hurt.
  4. Set up a small speaker that emits a sound of a bird that has spotted a predator when a motion senser is set up.
  5. Birds are highly aggravated by scented candles,the essential oils cause the birds nostrils and throat to be irritated.

Why?

Most common birds hold diseases that can infect you and in some cases,can be fatal.(here's a link to a website about bird diseases) https://www.rentokil.com/blog/bird-diseases. Birds also do things that are just plain annoying,like pooping on cars or even destroying crops.
