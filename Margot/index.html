~Plastic X-Ray~

A way to protect our oceans and sea creatures

About the Plastic X-Ray

The Plastic X-Ray was designed to scan your sea food to idetify any harmful plastic particles. This way you can tell if the food is safe besore you eat it. Also, when using the Plastic X-Ray, you can idetify where there are plastic deposits depending on the fish you are scanning. For example, if you scanned multiple salmon and if there are plastisc particles in them, you could infer that there are plastic or plastic particles in lakes or rivers.

Other Problems in the Ocean

There are many other problems in the health of our ocean too. There are oil spills and with global warming, coral reefs are dying. If we want a healthy ocean for the future we have to spread awarness and realize that there are ways to help.

Here are some ways you can help!

  • Reduce Electricity Use
  • Make Safe Substainable Sea Food Choices
  • Spread Awarness about Ocean Pollution
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle!
  • Use Refillable Water Bottles
  • Donate to Organiztions helping to Save our Oceans!
