Dialed Pillow

Zane Lyle

This invention is for when sometimes you can't sleep at night because your pillow is too hot. The dial on it leades to the cooling box in the back that reacts to the fabric.

The Most Important Features are:

  • The cooling box (controled by the dial(item 2))
  • The dial(leads to the cooling box)
  • The 2nd layer of the fabric(reacts to the cold from the cooling box)
  • The 1st layer of the fabric(has holes in it so you can feel the cooling)

Anyone who has the problem I mentioned before.
